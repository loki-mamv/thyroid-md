Asthma Resources

Tools, apps, and trusted organizations to help you manage asthma effectively.

⚠️ Severe Asthma Attack — Seek Emergency Care If:

• Rescue inhaler isn't helping or relief lasts less than 4 hours
• Breathing is rapid and labored, or you can't speak in full sentences
• Lips or fingernails turn blue
• Peak flow drops into red zone

Call 911 or go to the ER immediately
